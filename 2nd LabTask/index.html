OUR L.A. ROOTS

Founded by Herbert Hyman in 1963, The Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf opened its first store in Brentwood, Los Angeles, aiming to bring the best global coffee and tea to Southern California. Known for quality and excellence, they quickly became pioneers in California's gourmet coffee scene. Today, the core philosophy remains the same: crafting hand-made beverages with pride, from seed to cup.
